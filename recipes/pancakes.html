Pancakes

Description

Alright, listen up, because today we're tackling a breakfast classic that's so easy, it practically makes itself we're talking about those golden, fluffy delights known as pancakes, straight from the trusty Bisquick box recipe! Now, I know what you're thinking, "Chef, a box mix? Really?" And to that, I say, "Why mess with perfection, especially when perfection comes in a bright yellow box and saves you a ton of time?" We're going to transform this humble mix into stacks of pure joy with just a few simple additions. So grab your whisk, put on your most charming apron, and let's get flippin'!"

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Original Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Stir ingredients until blended.
  2. Pour slightly less than 1/4 cupfuls onto hot greased griddle.
  3. Cook until edges are dry; about 1 minute 30 seconds. Turn; cook 1 minute 30 seconds or until golden.
